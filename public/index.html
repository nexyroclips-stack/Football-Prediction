How it works: every team starts at 1500 Elo. After each logged result, ratings shift based on whether the outcome beat, matched, or missed expectation — bigger surprises move ratings more. Predictions convert the rating gap between two teams into win/draw/loss probabilities. With only a handful of results, this will look close to a coin flip — that's accurate, not broken. It gets sharper as the log grows.
